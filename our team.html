Our Team

Strengths

Being one of the few national research centers in South Sudan, led and managed by the national and regional researchers in the Nile basin, the center has an in-depth knowledge of their areas of academic and professional engagement in terms of a robust collaborative network at local, sub-national, national and regional levels with its stakeholders.

Weakness

Given the fact that NICAAR is a newly established climate and agricultural research center in South Sudan, it requires many efforts to strengthen and consolidate its existing technical, institutional, and human capacities as it strives to create a broader network of partners or stakeholders including the resource partners within the region and beyond.

Opportunities

Threats

Besides current waves of vulnerabilities, security, and climate-induced risk factors, the major threats to NICAAR’s operations also include an existing dynamic, challenging, and inconsistent socioeconomic, physical, and policy environment that may continue to jeopardize its program operations in the target areas.
